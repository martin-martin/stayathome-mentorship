Solidarity in the time of COVID-19

{% include 'intro.html' %}

Learn To Program For Free: Increasing your technical skills will not solve the economic and societal problems we are currently facing, but it can help to put yourself in a more stable position. In terms of financial stability, tech jobs are still some of the best paid and most in-demand positions in many places around the world, and they can give many people the opportunity to work remotely. Being able to create value remotely and getting paid even during a situation like the current COVID-19 lock-downs is a privilege that we would like to make as available as possible to affected workers.

If you lost your job due to the effects of this pandemic and are staying at home in self-isolation and you are considering a career transition into tech, apply here for free access to our course curriculum to learn Python, one of the most in-demand and easiest to learn programming languages, or Java, one of the most widely used programming languages in many industries.
At CodingNomads we know the value and importance of mentorship for learning to code, so we will aim to pair you with a pro-bono mentor to keep you accountable and check in with you while you study this new skill. Please keep in mind that mentors will give their free time to you, and we can't promise to keep this going, since it depends on the availability of individual people. But we'll do our best!
